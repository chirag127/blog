## 13. Topiramate (The Craving Killer)
**Salt Name:** Topiramate
**Indian Brands:** Topamac, Topamate, Topaz, Topirol, Epitomax
**Class:** Anticonvulsant / Anti-Epileptic
**Primary Use:** Epilepsy, Migraine prevention
**Weight Loss:** Side effect (very significant)
**Prescription Required:** Yes (Schedule H)
**Online Availability:** ✅ 1mg, Apollo, PharmEasy.

### How it Causes Weight Loss
Topiramate works on multiple brain channels simultaneously:
1. **GABA enhancement** → calms "food seeking" anxiety.
2. **Carbonic anhydrase inhibition** → alters taste perception (soda tastes flat, sweet things become unappealing).
3. **Glutamate blockade** → reduces the "reward" from eating junk food.
4. **Appetite center suppression** → less hunger overall.

It essentially rewires your relationship with food. Many patients report that they "just stop thinking about food" after 2–4 weeks.

### Dosage for Weight Loss
- Starting: 25mg at night (critical — start slow to minimize cognitive effects).
- Titrate up by 25mg every 1–2 weeks.
- Target: 100mg–200mg/day (split doses).
- Maximum: 400mg/day.

### Benefits
- 3–9 percent total body weight loss in trials.
- Kills "hedonic hunger" (eating for pleasure, not hunger).
- Migraine prevention bonus.
- Reduces alcohol cravings (being studied).
- Works well combined with Phentermine (Qsymia — not yet in India).

### Side Effects
| Side Effect | Frequency | What it Feels Like |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **"Brain Fog" / Word-finding difficulty** | **Common (15–20%)** | Can't remember words mid-sentence. "Dopamax effect." |
| Tingling in hands/feet (paresthesia) | Common (30–40%) | Pins and needles, especially at high doses. |
| Taste changes | Common | Carbonated drinks taste metallic. |
| Kidney stones | Uncommon (1.5%) | Calcium phosphate stones — drink lots of water. |
| Drowsiness | Common (start) | Usually resolves. |
| Depression/mood changes | Uncommon | Monitor carefully. |
| Metabolic acidosis | Rare | At high doses. |
| **Birth defects (Cleft palate)** | Rare | **NEVER use in pregnancy — Category D.** |
| Memory impairment | Uncommon | Higher doses. |

> Critical Warning: If you are a woman of childbearing age, you MUST use effective contraception while on Topiramate. It causes cleft palate and other birth defects. This is not optional.

> The "Dopamax" effect is real. Some people on 200mg+ feel cognitively slow. Start at 25mg and increase very slowly. Most people tolerate 50–100mg without significant cognitive impact.

### Verdict
**Rating: 8/10. ✅ YES (with supervision).**
One of the most effective cheap weight loss drugs available. The cognitive side effects are manageable at low doses. Work with a neurologist or endocrinologist.

## 14. Bupropion (The Food Noise Silencer)
**Salt Name:** Bupropion Hydrochloride
**Indian Brands:** Bupron SR, Zyban, Wellbutrin SR (imported), Bupropion-SR
**Class:** NDRI (Norepinephrine-Dopamine Reuptake Inhibitor) / Antidepressant
**Primary Use:** Depression, Smoking cessation
**Prescription Required:** Yes (Schedule H)
**Online Availability:** ✅ Available.

### How it Causes Weight Loss
Bupropion increases **dopamine and norepinephrine** in the brain. This:
1. Reduces the "reward value" of food (food becomes less exciting).
2. Suppresses appetite via norepinephrine.
3. Increases energy expenditure slightly.
4. Reduces the constant mental chatter about food ("food noise").

It is the antidepressant least likely to cause weight gain — in fact, it commonly causes weight loss.

### Dosage for Weight Loss
- Starting: 150mg SR (Sustained Release) once daily in the morning.
- Target: 150mg SR twice daily.
- **Never take at night** — severe insomnia.

## 17. Dapagliflozin (The Passive Calorie Flush)
**Salt Name:** Dapagliflozin
**Indian Brands:** Forxiga, Dapaglit, Dapaglide, Oxra, Dapamac
**Class:** SGLT2 Inhibitor / Anti-Diabetic
**Primary Use:** Diabetes, Heart failure, Kidney protection
**Online Availability:** ✅ Widely available.

### How it Causes Weight Loss
SGLT2 inhibitors block glucose reabsorption in the kidney. Instead of recycling glucose back into the blood, your body **dumps 60–90 grams of glucose per day into the urine** — that's approximately **200–350 calories per day** passively lost. No appetite suppression, no brain effects — just physics.

### Dosage
- 10mg once daily (standard).

### Benefits
- Passive 2–4 kg weight loss in 6 months.
- **Massive heart and kidney protection** — proven in landmark trials.
- Reduces blood pressure.
- No hypoglycemia risk (does not affect insulin directly).

### Side Effects
| Side Effect | Frequency | Management |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |

### Verdict
**Rating: 8.5/10. ✅ YES (excellent passive tool).**
One of the best mid-range additions. The heart and kidney benefits alone make it worth the cost. Generic versions are very affordable.

## 19. Orlistat (The Fat Blocker)
**Salt Name:** Orlistat
**Indian Brands:** Xenical (120mg), Alli (60mg OTC in some countries), Vyfat, Olistat, Adipocut, Ultislim, Lipocut, O-Stat
**Class:** Pancreatic Lipase Inhibitor
**Primary Use:** Weight Loss (this IS its main use)
**Prescription Required:** 120mg needs prescription; 60mg sometimes OTC

Orlistat blocks the pancreatic lipase enzyme that digests dietary fat. Approximately **30% of fat you eat passes through undigested**. This directly reduces calorie absorption from fat.

Effective in studies (5–10% weight loss). Tolerable only if you commit to a very low-fat diet. Useful if you need a non-systemic option (heart patients who can't take stimulants).

## 24. Phentermine (The Old-School Stimulant)

**Class:** Sympathomimetic Amine / CNS Stimulant
**Legal Status:** **Extremely Restricted / Schedule X**

### How it Works
Phentermine releases **norepinephrine** in the hypothalamus, strongly suppressing appetite within hours. It's the most prescribed weight loss drug in the USA but is **banned for long-term use**.

**Rating: ⚠️ HIGH RISK in India.**
