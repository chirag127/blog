## 6. 🧠 Topiramate (The Craving Killer)

**Salt Name:** Topiramate  
**Indian Brands:** Topamac, Topamate, Topaz, Topirol, Epitomax  
**Class:** Anticonvulsant / Anti-Epileptic  
**Primary Use:** Epilepsy, Migraine prevention  
**Weight Loss:** Side effect (very significant)  
**Prescription Required:** Yes (Schedule H)  
**Online Availability:** ✅ 1mg, Apollo, PharmEasy

### How it Causes Weight Loss
Topiramate works on multiple brain channels simultaneously:
1. **GABA enhancement** → calms "food seeking" anxiety
2. **Carbonic anhydrase inhibition** → alters taste perception (soda tastes flat, sweet things become unappealing)
3. **Glutamate blockade** → reduces the "reward" from eating junk food
4. **Appetite center suppression** → less hunger overall

It essentially rewires your relationship with food. Many patients report that they "just stop thinking about food" after 2–4 weeks.

### Dosage for Weight Loss
- Starting: 25mg at night (critical — start slow to minimize cognitive effects)
- Titrate up by 25mg every 1–2 weeks
- Target: 100mg–200mg/day (split doses)
- Maximum: 400mg/day

### ✅ Benefits

- Kills "hedonic hunger" (eating for pleasure, not hunger)
- Migraine prevention bonus
- Reduces alcohol cravings (being studied)
- Works well combined with Phentermine (Qsymia — not yet in India)

### ⚠️ Side Effects — Full Detail
| Side Effect | Frequency | What it Feels Like |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **"Brain Fog" / Word-finding difficulty** | **Common (15–20%)** | Can't remember words mid-sentence. "Dopamax effect." |
| Tingling in hands/feet (paresthesia) | Common (30–40%) | Pins and needles, especially at high doses |
| Taste changes | Common | Carbonated drinks taste metallic |
| Kidney stones | Uncommon (1.5%) | Calcium phosphate stones — drink lots of water |
| Drowsiness | Common (start) | Usually resolves |
| Depression/mood changes | Uncommon | Monitor carefully |
| Metabolic acidosis | Rare | At high doses |
| **Birth defects (Cleft palate)** | Rare | **NEVER use in pregnancy — Category D** |
| Memory impairment | Uncommon | Higher doses |

> ⚠️ **Critical Warning:** If you are a woman of childbearing age, you MUST use effective contraception while on Topiramate. It causes cleft palate and other birth defects. This is not optional.

> ⚠️ **The "Dopamax" effect** is real. Some people on 200mg+ feel cognitively slow. Start at 25mg and increase very slowly. Most people tolerate 50–100mg without significant cognitive impact.

### 🏥 Verdict
**Rating: 8/10. ✅ YES (with supervision).**  
One of the most effective cheap weight loss drugs available. The cognitive side effects are manageable at low doses. Work with a neurologist or endocrinologist.

## 7. 🎯 Bupropion (The Food Noise Silencer)

**Salt Name:** Bupropion Hydrochloride  
**Indian Brands:** Bupron SR, Zyban, Wellbutrin SR (imported), Bupropion-SR  
**Class:** NDRI (Norepinephrine-Dopamine Reuptake Inhibitor) / Antidepressant  
**Primary Use:** Depression, Smoking cessation  
**Prescription Required:** Yes (Schedule H)  
**Online Availability:** ✅ Available

### How it Causes Weight Loss
Bupropion increases **dopamine and norepinephrine** in the brain. This:
1. Reduces the "reward value" of food (food becomes less exciting)
2. Suppresses appetite via norepinephrine
3. Increases energy expenditure slightly
4. Reduces the constant mental chatter about food ("food noise")

It is the antidepressant least likely to cause weight gain — in fact, it commonly causes weight loss.

### Dosage for Weight Loss
- Starting: 150mg SR (Sustained Release) once daily in the morning
- Target: 150mg SR twice daily
- **Never take at night** — severe insomnia

| Bupropion 300mg XL | ₹130–₹250 | ₹13–₹25 |

**Monthly Cost (300mg/day SR):** ₹510–₹960

### ✅ Benefits
- Helps with emotional/stress eating
- Also helps quit smoking (bonus)
- No sexual dysfunction (unlike SSRIs)
- Good for depression-related weight gain

### ⚠️ Side Effects
| Side Effect | Frequency | Severity |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Insomnia | Common | Take only in morning |
| Dry mouth | Common | Mild |
| Headache | Common (first 2 weeks) | Mild |
| Anxiety, agitation | Common | Usually settles |
| Increased blood pressure | Uncommon | Monitor |
| **Seizures** | Rare (0.1% at 300mg) | **Do NOT use if history of seizures, eating disorder, or alcohol withdrawal** |
| Suicidal ideation | Rare (young adults) | FDA Black Box Warning |

### 🏥 Verdict
**Rating: 7.5/10. ✅ YES (for food-obsessed or emotional eaters).**  
Particularly effective if your weight gain is linked to depression, stress eating, or nicotine use. Combine with Topiramate (off-label — ask your doctor) for powerful synergistic effect.

## 8. 💊 Bupropion + Naltrexone (Contrave / Mysimba)

**Salt Names:** Bupropion HCl + Naltrexone HCl  
**Brands:** Contrave (USA), Mysimba (Europe) — **Not officially available in India**  
**Class:** Combination metabolic drug  
**Availability India:** ❌ Not officially marketed. Must get components separately on prescription.

### How it Works
- **Bupropion** (see above): Reduces reward value of food
- **Naltrexone**: Opioid antagonist — blocks the "pleasure" signal from eating. When the opioid receptor is blocked, food is less rewarding neurologically.

Together, they create a powerful suppression of the entire reward-eating cycle.

### Expected Results
- 5–9% weight loss at 1 year (clinical trials)
- Most effective for "hedonic obesity" — eating due to cravings rather than hunger

### Price in India (Components separately)
- Bupropion 150mg SR: ~₹10–₹16/tablet
- Naltrexone 50mg (Naltima): ~₹70–₹110/strip (10 tablets)

**Combined Monthly Cost:** ₹800–₹2,000

### ⚠️ Side Effects
- Nausea (very common at start — especially Naltrexone)
- Headache
- Constipation
- Insomnia
- Do NOT use in opioid users — precipitates severe withdrawal

### 🏥 Verdict
**Rating: 7/10. ✅ YES (if specific components are prescribed).**

## 9. 🩺 Duloxetine (Cymbalta)

**Salt Name:** Duloxetine Hydrochloride  
**Indian Brands:** Duzela, Cymbalta, Duloxetina, Duxet  
**Class:** SNRI (Serotonin-Norepinephrine Reuptake Inhibitor)  
**Primary Use:** Depression, Anxiety, Fibromyalgia, Diabetic nerve pain  
**Online Availability:** ✅ Available

### Weight Loss Mechanism
Dual reuptake inhibition increases satiety signaling. Also reduces pain-related inactivity (indirect benefit). Weight loss effect is mild and inconsistent — some patients gain weight long-term.

## 10. 💧 Dapagliflozin (The Passive Calorie Flush)

**Salt Name:** Dapagliflozin  
**Indian Brands:** Forxiga, Dapaglit, Dapaglide, Oxra, Dapamac  
**Class:** SGLT2 Inhibitor / Anti-Diabetic  
**Primary Use:** Diabetes, Heart failure, Kidney protection  
**Online Availability:** ✅ Widely available

### How it Causes Weight Loss
SGLT2 inhibitors block glucose reabsorption in the kidney. Instead of recycling glucose back into the blood, your body **dumps 60–90 grams of glucose per day into the urine** — that's approximately **200–350 calories per day** passively lost. No appetite suppression, no brain effects — just physics.

### Dosage
- 10mg once daily (standard)

### Price in India (April 2026)
| Brand | Price per Strip (10 tabs) | Per Tablet |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Forxiga 10mg (Branded) | ₹350–₹450 | ₹35–₹45 |
| Dapaglit 10mg (Generic) | ₹80–₹150 | ₹8–₹15 |
| Oxra 10mg | ₹100–₹180 | ₹10–₹18 |

**Monthly Cost:** ₹240–₹1,350 (depending on brand)

### ✅ Benefits
- Passive 2–4 kg weight loss in 6 months
- **Massive heart and kidney protection** — proven in landmark trials
- Reduces blood pressure
- No hypoglycemia risk (does not affect insulin directly)

### ⚠️ Side Effects
| Side Effect | Frequency | Management |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| UTI (urinary tract infection) | Common (5-10%) | Drink lots of water |
| Genital yeast infections | Common (women) | Antifungal cream |
| Increased urination | Common | Expected — normal |
| Dehydration / low BP | Uncommon | Drink water, monitor |
| DKA (diabetic ketoacidosis) | Rare | Serious — stop if sick/fasting |
| Fournier's Gangrene | Very Rare | Genital area — seek emergency care |
| Leg amputation risk | Rare (Canagliflozin mainly) | Less concern with Dapagliflozin |

> ⚠️ **Warning:** During illness, surgery, or prolonged fasting, SGLT2 inhibitors can cause **euglycemic DKA** — a type of diabetic crisis with NORMAL blood sugar. Always stop the drug before any planned surgery.

### 🏥 Verdict
**Rating: 8.5/10. ✅ YES (excellent passive tool).**  
One of the best mid-range additions. The heart and kidney benefits alone make it worth the cost. Generic versions are very affordable.

## 12. 🔴 Orlistat (The Fat Blocker)

**Salt Name:** Orlistat  
**Indian Brands:** Xenical (120mg), Alli (60mg OTC in some countries), Vyfat, Olistat  
**Class:** Pancreatic Lipase Inhibitor  
**Primary Use:** Weight Loss (this IS its main use)  
**Prescription Required:** 120mg needs prescription; 60mg sometimes OTC  
**Online Availability:** ✅ Available

### How it Works
Orlistat blocks the pancreatic lipase enzyme that digests dietary fat. Approximately **30% of fat you eat passes through undigested**. This directly reduces calorie absorption from fat.

### Dosage
- 60mg with each main meal (OTC dose)
- 120mg with each main meal (prescription dose)
- Take ONLY with meals that contain fat. Skip if meal is fat-free.

### Price in India (April 2026)
| Formulation | Price per Strip (10 caps) | Per Capsule |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Orlistat 60mg (Vyfat) | ₹85–₹180 | ₹8.5–₹18 |
| Orlistat 120mg (Xenical) | ₹250–₹500 | ₹25–₹50 |

**Monthly Cost (120mg TDS):** ₹750–₹1,500

### ⚠️ Side Effects — THE HONEST TRUTH
| Side Effect | Frequency | What Actually Happens |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Oily/fatty stools** | **Very Common (20–40%)** | Orange, oily discharge in underwear |
| **Oily spotting** | **Very Common** | Uncontrolled oily leakage |
| **Fecal urgency** | Common | Sudden, urgent need to go |
| Vitamin A, D, E, K deficiency | Common | Must supplement fat-soluble vitamins |
| Liver damage | Very Rare | Monitor if long-term use |

> ⚠️ **The Orlistat Reality Check:** Most people who eat a high-fat diet while on Orlistat experience **"anal leakage"** — uncontrolled oily discharge from the rectum, often orange-colored. You will want to wear dark pants. The "treatment" is: eat low fat. Which is exactly what you should have done without the drug.

### 🏥 Verdict
**Rating: 5/10. ⚖️ MAYBE (only with strict low-fat diet).**  
Effective in studies (5–10% weight loss). Tolerable only if you commit to a very low-fat diet. Useful if you need a non-systemic option (heart patients who can't take stimulants).

## 13. 😴 Zonisamide

**Salt Name:** Zonisamide  
**Indian Brands:** Zonisep, Zonimid, Zenoxa, Zonidet  
**Class:** Anticonvulsant  
**Primary Use:** Epilepsy  
**Online Availability:** ✅ Available (less common)

Works similarly to Topiramate (carbonic anhydrase inhibitor + sodium channel blocker). Causes appetite suppression and weight loss. Often used as a Topiramate alternative.

### Price in India

| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 50mg | ₹60–₹150 | ₹6–₹15 |
| 100mg | ₹100–₹250 | ₹10–₹25 |

**Monthly Cost:** ₹360–₹750

- Drowsiness, dizziness
- **Kidney stones** (same as Topiramate — drink water!)
- Decreased sweating (hyperthermia risk in hot weather — relevant in India)
- Cognitive slowing (less than Topiramate)

**Verdict: ⚖️ MAYBE. 6.5/10** — Good Topiramate alternative but less studied for weight loss specifically.
