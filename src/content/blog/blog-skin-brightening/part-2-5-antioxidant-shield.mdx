# Part 2.5: The Antioxidant Shield

In the previous parts, we learned how to block pigment and remove dead skin. Now, we learn how to **protect** that brightness. In the Indian climate, UV rays and pollution generate "Free Radicals" that darken skin instantly. Antioxidants are your skin's personal bodyguards.

These are ranked by their **"Efficacy" (Efficacy Rank #24 to #29)**.

---

## 🏆 Efficacy Rank #24: Vitamin C (L-Ascorbic Acid)
**The "Radiance Queen"**

L-Ascorbic Acid is the pure, most active form of Vitamin C.

### How it Works
It is a multi-tasker. It neutralizes free radicals, boosts collagen, and inhibits tyrosinase. In Indian skin, it provides that "lit-from-within" glow that no other ingredient can quite match. However, it is very unstable and "oxidizes" (turns brown) quickly in the Indian heat.

### 💰 Price & Brands in India (2025)
*   **Premium**: *Vichy LiftActiv Vitamin C*, *La Roche-Posay Pure Vitamin C10*.
    *   **Price**: ₹2,500 – ₹3,500.
*   **Indian Pharma**: *Envita C* (Micro Labs).
    *   **Price**: ₹600 – ₹900.
*   **Jan Aushadhi Kendra**:
    *   **Availability**: Mostly available as **Vitamin C 500mg Tablets** (₹15 for 10 tabs). Eating Vitamin C helps your skin from the inside! Topical serums are rare in Jan Aushadhi but are being introduced in metro branches.

---

## 🥈 Efficacy Rank #25: Ethyl Ascorbic Acid (EAA)
**The "Indian Favorite"**

Because pure Vitamin C is so unstable, most Indian brands (like Minimalist and Plum) use EAA.

### Why it's Great for India
EAA is 86% pure Vitamin C but is **extremely stable**. It doesn't turn brown in the Mumbai humidity or the Delhi heat. It penetrates the skin deeply and converts into Vitamin C inside.

### 💰 Price & Brands in India
*   **Brands**: *Minimalist 16% Vitamin C*, *Plum 15% Vitamin C Glow Boost*.
    *   **Price**: ₹550 – ₹700.
*   **Jan Aushadhi**: **Not available**.

---

## 🥉 Efficacy Rank #26: Sodium Ascorbyl Phosphate (SAP)
**The "Acne-Safe Brightener"**

SAP is a salt form of Vitamin C.

### Why it's Great for India
Unlike L-Ascorbic Acid, which can be irritating, SAP is very gentle. More importantly, studies show it has **anti-microbial properties** against acne-causing bacteria. For Indians with oily, acne-prone skin, this is the best Vitamin C choice.

### 💰 Price & Brands in India
*   **Brands**: *Mamaearth Skin Illuminate Face Serum*.
    *   **Price**: ₹450 – ₹600.

---

## 4. Resveratrol
**Efficacy Rank: #27 — The Long-Term Protector**

Derived from red grapes, Resveratrol is a "Sirtuin activator."

### How it Works
It works at a cellular level to protect skin from the "grey" look caused by pollution (PM2.5). In Indian metros, using Resveratrol at night is the best way to wake up with bright skin.

### 💰 Price & Brands in India
*   **Brands**: *Minimalist 1% Resveratrol + Ferulic Acid*.
    *   **Price**: ₹500 – ₹700.

---

## 5. Ferulic Acid
**Efficacy Rank: #28 — The "Booster"**

Ferulic acid is rarely used alone. It is almost always paired with Vitamin C and E.

### Why it's Great for India
It doubles the photo-protection of Vitamin C. If your Vitamin C serum doesn't have Ferulic Acid, it is working half as hard as it could be in the Indian sun.

---

## 6. Vitamin E (Tocopherol)
**Efficacy Rank: #29 — The Repairer**

### 💰 Price & Brands in India
*   **Pharma**: *Evion 400* (Merck). This is a household name in India.
    *   **Price**: ₹30 – ₹50 for a strip of 10 capsules.
    *   Many Indians prick these capsules and mix them with their creams. This is great for dry skin but can cause breakouts in oily skin.
