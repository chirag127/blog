# Part 2.4: The Surface Renewers

If you have used the blockers from the previous chapters but your skin still looks "dull," it’s likely because you have a buildup of dead, pigmented skin cells on the surface. In this chapter, we rank the **Exfoliants**. 

In the Indian climate, dust and pollution stick to the natural oils on our face, creating a "grey" layer. These ingredients dissolve the "glue" holding that layer together, revealing the bright skin underneath.

These are ranked by their **"Efficacy" (Efficacy Rank #18 to #23)**.

---

## 🏆 Efficacy Rank #18: Glycolic Acid (AHA)
**The "Surface Powerhouse"**

Derived from sugarcane, Glycolic Acid has the smallest molecular size of all AHAs, allowing it to penetrate the deepest.

### How it Works
It dissolves the bonds between dead skin cells. In Indian skin, it is excellent for removing a sun tan and smoothing out rough texture. However, because it penetrates deeply, it can be irritating.

### 💰 Price & Brands in India (2025)
*   **Pharma**: *Glyco-6* or *Glyco-12* (Micro Labs). This is a cult favourite among Indian dermatologists.
    *   **Price**: ₹200 – ₹350 for 30g.
*   **Cosmetic**: *Minimalist 8% Glycolic Acid Liquid Exfoliant*.
    *   **Price**: ₹450 – ₹600.
*   **Jan Aushadhi Kendra**:
    *   **Availability**: High (Generic Glycolic Acid 6% Cream).
    *   **Price**: **₹40 – ₹60**.

---

## 🥈 Efficacy Rank #19: Lactic Acid (AHA)
**The "Gentle Hydrator"**

Originally found in sour milk (and the secret to Cleopatra's legendary baths), Lactic Acid is a larger molecule than Glycolic.

### How it Works
It exfoliates the surface but also acts as a **Humectant** (it pulls water into the skin). For Indians with dry skin or those living in North India's dry winters, Lactic Acid is superior to Glycolic. It provides a "glow" without the stinging.

### 💰 Price & Brands in India
*   **Brands**: *The Ordinary Lactic Acid 10%*, *Minimalist Lactic Acid 10%*.
    *   **Price**: ₹600 – ₹800.
*   **Jan Aushadhi**: Often found in combination moisturizing lotions.
    *   **Price**: **₹30 – ₹50**.

---

## 🥉 Efficacy Rank #20: Salicylic Acid (BHA)
**The "Pore Deep-Cleaner"**

If your dullness is caused by oily skin and clogged pores, AHAs won't help. You need a BHA.

### How it Works
Salicylic Acid is oil-soluble. It goes *inside* the pore to dissolve sebum and dead skin. For Indian teenagers and adults with acne-prone skin, this is the #1 ingredient to prevent the dark marks (PIH) before they even start.

### 💰 Price & Brands in India (2025)
*   **Pharma**: *Saslic* / *Saslic DS* (Cipla) Foaming Face Wash.
    *   **Price**: ₹300 – ₹500.
*   **Jan Aushadhi Kendra**:
    *   **Availability**: Very High (Generic Salicylic Acid Foaming Face Wash).
    *   **Price**: **₹45 – ₹70**. (Incredible savings compared to Saslic).

## 4. Mandelic Acid
**Efficacy Rank: #21 — The Safety Champion**

Derived from bitter almonds, Mandelic Acid has a very large molecular size.

### Why it's Great for India
Because it is a large molecule, it penetrates the skin **slowly**. This means it is very unlikely to cause the "Post-Inflammatory Hyperpigmentation" that sometimes happens if you use Glycolic Acid too aggressively on dark Indian skin.

### 💰 Price & Brands in India
*   **Brands**: *Minimalist 10% Mandelic Acid*.
    *   **Price**: ₹500 – ₹700.

---

## 5. PHAs (Gluconolactone / Lactobionic Acid)
**Efficacy Rank: #22 — The Sensitive Skin Solution**

PHAs are the "cousins" of AHAs. They are ultra-gentle.

### 💰 Price & Brands in India
*   **Brands**: *The Derma Co 7% PHA Gentle Face Toner*.
    *   **Price**: ₹400 – ₹600.

---

## 6. Papain (Papaya Enzyme)
**Efficacy Rank: #23 — The Natural Exfoliant**

Found in Papaya fruit, this is an enzyme that "eats" dead skin cells. It is less predictable than acids but very popular in traditional Indian face packs.
